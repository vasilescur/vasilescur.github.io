OUR MISSION

To give back to the community by raising awareness about the benefits of public school music programs. To foster creativity, imagination, development, engagement, discipline, teamwork, self-confidence, and success in students of all ages through increased availability and participation in public school music programs. To actively be a part of the change we wish to see, by teaching, performing, and inspiring young students of music.
